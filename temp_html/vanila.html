    Vanila Hotel & Spa

The Vanila Hotel & Spa is a 4-star hotel of the Sound's Hotels group located by the sea on the west coast of Nosy Be, is opposite Nosy Sakatia, "the orchid island".

With a typical Malagasy architecture, the Vanila is located in a lush tropical park that fits perfectly into the original landscape of the island of perfumes.

Book your stay

AUTHENTICITY

Renovated in 2022, the Vanila Hotel & Spa welcomes you to an authentic place showcasing Malagasy culture in the heart of Nosy Be, the island of perfumes; its surrounding islets, its exotic fauna and flora come together to make this setting a well-preserved paradise. The hotel has 2 swimming pools with a breathtaking view of Nosy Sakatia, an access to the beach, a spa, a restaurant and many other facilities thought and designed especially for your well-being in order to make your stay unforgettable.

NATURE

Between palm trees, ravinala and other endemic plants of Madagascar, the Vanila Hotel & Spa welcomes you in a green setting. With typical Malagasy architecture, this 4-star hotel (international standards) is located in a lush tropical park that fits perfectly into the original landscape of the island of perfumes and which will guarantee you a total change of scenery.

DISCOVERY

Madagascar is one of the last places in the world to have kept its authenticity with its unique way of life, its varied landscapes, its endemic fauna and flora. Adventure, escape, intimacy, diversity… So many words could describe Madagascar, but the real experience does not pass through words, but through feelings. We say "Feel the Sound's", an expression that can only be understood by the few people who have already experienced a trip to Madagascar. Our goal is to offer sensations and emotions more than simple activities in Nosy Be and its surroundings.

OUR BEST EXCURSIONS

For several years now, our partner Sound's Travel & Leisure has been offering our customers tailor-made excursions and tours.

Discover Malagasy culture with Sound's Travel and Leisure, a destination management company offering exclusive travel arrangements across Madagascar.

Our excursions are developed in collaboration with Sound's Travel & Leisure to provide the most suitable and comfortable experiences for our customers.

The 3 islands

Nosy Komba - Nosy Tanikely - Nosy Sakatia

This full day tour gives the opportunity to visit a part of Nosy Be archipelago ; walks around local villages (Ampagorina), craft shops, lemurs environment and other species in Nosy Komba allow an authentic exploration of the malagasy culture.

More details

Nosy Iranja

Nosy Iranja is located at 30 miles away from Nosy Be. An entire day is necessary to discover this island and benefit from the spectacle of rising tide that covers the sand path of 2 km linking two islands (Nosy Iranja Be and Nosy Iranja Kely – which means the big Nosy Iranja and the little one).

More details

Island tour

Nosy Be

From the Hell Ville market, through the ylang ylang distillery to the sunset at Mount Passo, this full-day jeep excursion is rich in emotions thanks to the diversity of the landscapes visited and the discovery of the real local culture followed by its legends.
